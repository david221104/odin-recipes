Giros recipe for free

Description:

Giros is one of the most important Mediterranean dishes.

It originates from Greece, and is similar to other Eastern

Mediterranean foods, like shwarma.

Here is the free recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Pitas
  • Tzaziki sauce
  • Tomato
  • French fries
  • Chicken breasts

Here is how to create giors:

  1. Slice a chicken breasts to very small pieces
  2. Add some oil to the pan and fry the chicken for 10 minutes
  3. Add a tzatziki on your pita
  4. Add sliced tomatoes over it
  5. Put the chicken and after that some french fries on top
  6. Wrap your pita up, and enjoy!
