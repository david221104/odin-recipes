Pizza recipe for free

Description:

Pizza is one of the most beautiful dishes ever.

It was created in the 19th century by Italians,

and is one of the most well-known dishes in the world.

Today we will present to you a free recipe so that you

can enjoy in this marvelous food in the comfort of your home.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Salami

Steps to create pizza:

  1. Make the dough and use some olive oil
  2. After you created the dough, put tomato sauce over it
  3. Put whole mozzarella pieces on top of the sauce
  4. Now put some salami over it
  5. Bake it at 350 degrees for 5 minutes
  6. Voila! Enjoy the best meal in the world!
